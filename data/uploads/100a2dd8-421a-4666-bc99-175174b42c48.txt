# Test

Abstract
This is a test research paper about digital transformation in Indian SMEs. It uses mixed methods.

Introduction
Digital transformation is important for business competitiveness. SMEs form the backbone of the Indian economy.

Methodology
This study uses a mixed-methods approach with surveys of 200 SMEs across India.

Results
Findings show positive correlation between digital maturity and operational efficiency.

Conclusion
Digital transformation positively impacts SME performance. Future research should examine long-term impacts.
