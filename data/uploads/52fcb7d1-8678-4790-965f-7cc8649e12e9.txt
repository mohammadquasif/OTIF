# Test Paper

Abstract
This is a test research paper about digital transformation in Indian SMEs.

Introduction
Digital transformation is important for business competitiveness.

Methodology
This study uses a mixed-methods approach with surveys of 200 SMEs.

Results
Findings show positive correlation between digital maturity and efficiency.

Conclusion
Digital transformation positively impacts SME performance.
