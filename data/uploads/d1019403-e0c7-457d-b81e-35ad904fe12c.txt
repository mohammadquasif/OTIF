# Test Paper

## Abstract
Digital transformation impact on SME performance in India.

## Introduction
SMEs form backbone of Indian economy contributing 30% to GDP.

## Methods
Survey of 200 SMEs across India using mixed methods.

## Results
78% response rate. Digital maturity correlates with revenue growth.

## Conclusion
Digital transformation positively impacts SME performance.
