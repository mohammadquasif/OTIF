# Test Paper

## Abstract
Digital transformation impact on SME performance.

## Introduction
SMEs form backbone of Indian economy. Digital technologies critical.

## Methods
Survey of 200 SMEs across India. Mixed methods approach.

## Results
78 percent response rate. Digital maturity correlates with revenue.

## Conclusion
Digital transformation positively impacts SME performance.
